Spider-Man

PG   |   2h 16min   |   Adventure, Comedy   |   17 dec 2021 (USA)

Spider-Man has spider-like abilities including superhuman strength and the ability to cling to most surfaces. He is also extremely agile and has amazing reflexes. Spider-Man also has a “spider sense,” that warns him of impending danger. ... In the recent storyline, Spider-Man has been reborn with even stronger abilities.
